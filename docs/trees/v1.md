2) Data Links (v1)

/auth ↔ profiles, roles, user_roles

/dashboard ↔ children (ملخصات), growth_metrics (آخر قياس), analytics_daily (اختياري)

/children ↔ children (CRUD للولي), growth_metrics (عرض آخر قياس)

/child/:childId/overview ↔ children, growth_metrics, visits (آخر), diet_plans (أحدث حالة), attachments (count)

/child/:childId/readings ↔ glucose_readings, insulin_doses (سياقي), meals (ربط related_meal_id)

/child/:childId/meals[/*] ↔ meals, meal_items, food_items, food_portions, (اختياري) insulin_doses.linked_bolus_id

/child/:childId/visits[/*] ↔ visits, visit_recommendations, attachments

/child/:childId/plans[/*] ↔ diet_plans, diet_plan_meals, child_diabetes_profiles, (اختياري) carb_* / basal_* / mdi_basal_*

/child/:childId/files[/*] ↔ attachments (Bucket: attachments)

/food-items[/*] ↔ food_items, food_categories, food_portions (Bucket: food-items)

/meals (مجمّع) ↔ meals (حسب فلاتر الطفل), meal_items

/visits (مجمّع) ↔ visits (حسب فلاتر الطفل/المزوّد)

/analytics ↔ analytics_daily + تجميعات من meals/meal_items/glucose_readings

/doctor/child/:childId/ ↔ doctor_children, visits (CRUD للدكتور), glucose_readings (قراءة), meals (قراءة)

/dietitian/child/:childId/ ↔ dietitian_children, diet_plans/diet_plan_meals (CRUD للأخصائي), meals/meal_items (قراءة)

/admin ↔ profiles, roles, user_roles, doctor_children, dietitian_children, food_categories

القاعدة الذهبية: الواجهة تظهر/تخفي القدرات حسب الدور؛ RLS هو الحارس الحقيقي في قاعدة البيانات.
